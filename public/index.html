About Us

Welcome to Manan Manthan School of Indian Dance, where tradition meets expression. At our academy, we celebrate the rich heritage of Indian dance forms—blending classical grace with modern vibrance. Whether you're stepping into rhythm for the first time or refining your artistry, we offer a nurturing space to grow, learn, and shine. Our experienced instructors are passionate about passing on the timeless beauty of Kathak, Bharatanatyam, Folk, and fusion dance styles. We don’t just teach dance—we build confidence, discipline, and a lifelong love for movement. Join our vibrant community and let your journey of self-expression begin. With Manan and Manthan—feel the beat, find your soul.
